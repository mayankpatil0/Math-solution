Assertion & Reason Question

Assertion (A): The product of a rational number and its reciprocal is always 1 (including 0).

Reason (R): Every non-zero rational number has a reciprocal.

Correct Option: (d) Assertion is false, but Reason is true.

Explanation:

  • Assertion (A) is False: Zero (0) is a rational number, but it has no reciprocal because dividing by zero (1/0) is undefined. Therefore, 0 cannot multiply with a reciprocal to give 1.
  • Reason (R) is True: Every non-zero rational number has a well-defined reciprocal, and their product is always 1.
